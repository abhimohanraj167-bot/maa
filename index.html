My Lovely Maa 💖

Maa ke bina zindagi adhoori lagti hai,
Unki har dua mein khushiyan basi hoti hai,
Chahe duniya saath de ya na de,
Maa ki mohabbat hamesha saath hoti hai 💕
Maa, aap meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat hissa ho. Aapki muskaan meri sabse badi khushi hai aur aapka saath meri sabse badi taqat. Aapne hamesha mujhe samjha, support kiya aur bina kisi shart ke pyaar diya. Main shayad kabhi words mein express na kar paun, par aap mere liye sab kuch ho. I love you Maa ❤️
